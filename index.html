The Awful Leaders Project

What is the Awful Leaders project?

The economic downturn of 2022-2023 has revealed awful thinking and leadership style behind some of the corporate leaders worldwide. During the next boom cycle, some of these leaders will start to recruit again, and very likely change their tune when the pendulum swings in the favor of workers. Some of us would like to remember who all the awful leaders are so that we can ensure to never work for them! We do not wish to enable them further!

This project aims to be an Awesome-style public directory of awful leaders. Everyone is welcome to contribute.
